Monstera Deliciosa

Care Information
Monstera deliciosa, commonly called split-leaf philodendron, is native to Central America. It is a climbing, evergreen perennial vine that is perhaps most noted for its large perforated leaves on thick plant stems and its long cord-like aerial roots.
Sun Part Shade
Water Medium
Maintenance Low
Poisonous to Pets? No
